Sophomore Internships

Sophomore year is when you can start securing more substantial internships with hands-on experience.

Tax Preparation Intern

Assist with tax return preparation during busy season, working with individual and small business clients.

Seasonal

Bookkeeping Assistant

Help maintain financial records, process transactions, and assist with month-end closing procedures.

Year-Round

Audit Support Intern

Support audit teams with documentation, testing, and client communication under supervision.

Spring/Summer

Tips for Sophomores:

  • Develop proficiency in Excel and accounting software
  • Build relationships with professors for recommendations
  • Start preparing for CPA exam requirements
