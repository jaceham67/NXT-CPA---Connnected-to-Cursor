Tips for Sophomores:

  • Complete introductory accounting courses
  • Develop proficiency in Excel and accounting software
  • Build relationships with professors for recommendations
  • Start preparing for CPA exam requirements
  • Research diversity programs if you qualify - many firms offer these programs
  • Apply early - internship deadlines are often in the fall
