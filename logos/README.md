## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (with transparent background preferred)
- **Recommended Size**: 200-300px wide (height will auto-adjust)
- **Aspect Ratio**: Maintain original logo proportions
- **Background**: Transparent or white

## Where to Find Logos

You can find official logos from:
- Company websites (usually in their press/media sections)
- Logo databases like LogoVector.com, LogosDownload.com, or Brandfetch
- Company brand guidelines pages

## Fallback Behavior

If a logo image is not found, the firm name will be displayed as text instead. This ensures the scrolling section always works, even if some logos are missing.
