Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

NXT CPA collects information to improve user experience and provide relevant content. We use the following services that may collect data:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • How you arrived at our website (search engines, direct links, etc.)
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information

This information is collected anonymously and aggregated. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Form Submissions

When you submit a company through our "Submit a Company" form, we collect:

  • Internship level (Freshman, Sophomore, or Both)
  • Company name
  • Optional: Your name and email address (if provided)

This information is sent to jdenham@falcon.bentley.edu via Formspree for review purposes only. We do not share this information with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

  • To improve website content and user experience
  • To understand which pages are most useful to visitors
  • To review and potentially add company submissions to our listings
  • To respond to inquiries (if contact information is provided)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Data collected through Google Analytics is subject to Google's privacy practices. Form submissions are only accessible to the website administrator.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies through Google Analytics to track website usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external company websites and career pages. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any website you visit.

Disclaimer

This website is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any of the companies listed. All company information is publicly available and provided for informational purposes only.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were last made.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at jdenham@falcon.bentley.edu.
