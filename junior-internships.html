Junior Internships

Junior year internships are critical - many lead to full-time job offers upon graduation.

Big 4 Summer Internship

Intensive 8-10 week programs at Deloitte, PwC, EY, or KPMG with potential for full-time offers.

Competitive

Regional Firm Internship

Work with mid-size firms offering diverse client exposure and strong mentorship opportunities.

Summer

Corporate Finance Intern

Gain experience in corporate accounting departments, working on financial reporting and analysis.

Summer

Tips for Juniors:

  • Maintain a GPA of 3.5 or higher
  • Complete intermediate and advanced accounting courses
  • Attend firm recruiting events and office visits
  • Prepare for interviews with case studies and technical questions
  • Consider pursuing CPA exam credits if eligible
